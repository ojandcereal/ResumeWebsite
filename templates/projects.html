  • 2022

    Medical Assistance Box

    This was a project that involved a large amount of market research and engineering processes. Our objective was to assist some of the 20 million Americans that live with a chronic illness.

    We used Arduino to wire several chips, keypads, a breadboard, a buzzer, an LCD screen and buttons.

    The capabilities of this box include:

    • Entering the name of a user
    • Entering the name of a medication
    • Three separate times for reminders
    • Three different types of reminders
    • Reminder if medication has
      already been taken

    Skills:

    • Arduino
    • C++
    • Engineering
    • Electrical Circuits
    • Market Research
